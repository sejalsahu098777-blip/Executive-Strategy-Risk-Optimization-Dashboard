# Executive Strategy & 360 Business Risk Optimization Dashboard

## 📊 Project Overview
This repository features a comprehensive **Executive Strategy Dashboard** built in Power BI. It focuses on enterprise-level performance metrics, tracking macroeconomic revenue drivers, profit contributions, and shipping/risk pipelines to support data-driven decision-making for executive leadership.

## 💡 Executive Insights & Key Metrics (KPIs)
With a robust financial framework, the enterprise has generated **$1.35M in Total Revenue** with an impressive **25.00% Profit Margin%**, yielding a **Net Profit of $338.61K**.

### 🔍 Strategic Takeaways:
* **Revenue Drivers:** A waterfall analysis reveals significant growth contributions from the UK market and key segments like Beverages ($131K) and Confections ($101K).
* **Profit Contribution by Category:** **Beverages** stand as the highest profit contributor at **21.15% ($71.63K)**, followed closely by Dairy Products at 18.56%.
* **Vendor Efficiency:** United Package leads as the top-performing logistics partner by profit, securing **$103K**, showcasing maximum operational efficiency.
* **Risk & Operations Pipeline:** Currently monitoring **21 Pending Shipments** to mitigate operational delays and protect consumer satisfaction index levels.
